Image enhancement via log-modified histogram equalization and Laplacian pyramid

Submitted

Abstract: In this paper, a simple yet effective image enhancement algorithm is proposed to improve both global contrast and local details. Firstly, in the classic histogram equalization framework, the global contrast enhancement is achieved by simply modifying the image histogram with logarithmic operation. Due to the character of compression and monotonically increasing of logarithm, the improved histogram equalization can generate naturally looking global enhanced image without introducing visual artifacts. Secondly, to further improve local details, we adopt the Laplacian pyramid to decompose the global enhanced image into several detail layers at different scales. The final result is obtained by adding all pyramid layers with a weighted strategy. Experiments show that our efficient algorithm generates comparable or even better results with state-of-the-art 1D and 2D histogram based methods.
